Tools for moments of need

When emotions feel overwhelming, these gentle practices can help anchor you back to the present moment. Take what you need.

Quick Calm

Guided Breathing

Controlled breathing activates your parasympathetic nervous system, signaling safety to your brain. Follow the circle—breathe in as it expands, out as it contracts.

4

Inhale

Breathe in slowly through your nose for 4 counts

7

Hold

Gently hold your breath for 7 counts

8

Exhale

Release slowly through your mouth for 8 counts

Self-Care

Gentle Daily Practices

Small acts of care accumulate into profound healing. Select what resonates today.

Morning Light

Spend 10 minutes near natural light within an hour of waking to regulate your circadian rhythm.

Hydration

Drink one full glass of water slowly. Notice the temperature, the sensation, the nourishment.

Grounding Walk

Five minutes of walking, feeling your feet contact the earth. No destination required.
